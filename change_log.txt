= 1.0.1.6 =
- Added support for Gravity Forms Add-Ons build on the Gravity Forms Payment Add-On Framework. The form submission step will now wait until the payment has been processed before allowing the entry to continue to the next step.
- Added translations for Chinese (China) and Dutch (Netherlands).

- Added integration with the Gravity Flow Parent-Child Forms Extension; a parent form can now be selected for the 'Entry ID Field' setting on the 'Update an Entry' step.
